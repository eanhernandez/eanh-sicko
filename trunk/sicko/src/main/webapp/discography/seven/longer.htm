Recorded and Mixed by Greg Heino and Sicko, Sometime in early 1992

This was Top Drawer Records' first release - our song is an early recording of 'fB Song'...I think we recorded it with our friend Greg in the basement of our practice space; he knew marginally more about recording than we did. A later version of the song was released on the Count Me Out 7" on eMpTy Records.

The other bands and songs on this comp were:

Shag Big Block
Bone Cellar Support the World
Sore Jackson Unable Stump
