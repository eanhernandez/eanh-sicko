Okay, so here lies the output of our band during the eight years we were together. Our first recording was done in Ean's dank basement in the summer of 1991. We did it on some Tascam machine which recorded directly to cassette. The quality was marginal, but we made a demo tape out of it. One of the cassettes and a bottle of scotch made its way (courtesy of Denny's sister) to the house of the head of eMpTy Records, Blake Wright. Soon after, through some weird twist of fate, we were in the studio with our guitar rock hero, Kurt Bloch, recording our first single for the eMpTy Records World Empire. True story. Since then it's more of the same as you'll see.
